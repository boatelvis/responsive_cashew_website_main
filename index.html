Heart Health

Cashews are rich in unsaturated fats - a category of fats linked to a lower risk of premature death and heart diseases.

Antioxidants

Cashew is a rich source of polyphenols and carotenoids - these antioxidants increase the body's ability to stay healthy.

Brain Health

Statistics

This statistics shows the production of cashew nuts (in shell) in some leading African countries. In 2020, approximately 4.18 million metric tons of in shell basis cashew nuts were produced woorldwide, with Africa exceeding over 2.1 million metric tons of global production.

Ivory Coast

731,917 Metric Tons

Since 2014, Ivory Coast cashew nuts production grew 5.9% year on year. At 731,917 Metri Tons in 2019, the country was ranked number 3 comparing other countries in cashew nuts production.

Mali

149,890 Metric Tons

Since 2014, Mali cashew nuts production grew 15.8% year on year. At 149,890 Metric Tons in 2019, the country was ranked number 8 among other countries in cashew nuts production.

Burkina Faso

109,303 Metric Tons

Since 2014, Burkina Faso cashew nuts production jumped by 4%. At 109,303 Metric Tons in 2019, the country was ranked number 12 comparing other countries in cashew nuts production.

Ghana

81,770 Metric Tons

Since 2014, Ghana cashew nuts production rose 10.3% year on year. At 81,770 Metric Tons in 2019, the country was ranked number 14 comparing other countries in cashew nuts production.
